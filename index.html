Protecting Children in the Roblox Community

Our mission is to ensure that every child can enjoy the Roblox platform in a safe, supportive, and positive environment. We believe that gaming should inspire creativity and connection—not fear or exploitation.

About Us

We were founded with passion to achieve the goal of protecting children and fostering a safe envoirment within Roblox. We believe that all children deserve an envoirment free of harassment and abuse.

Our Initiatives

We organize events in Roblox such as protests, gatherings, memorials and other get-togethers to promote our cause.

Education Programs

We attempt to provide resources to parents and children for before and after-assault situations. We also aim to raise awareness of our cause.

Collaboration with Other groups

Our team works closely with other groups to insure safety in a wide area of Roblox, making this platform the best platform.

Support and Advocacy

We recommend, if a children as been, or is being harassed, to go to your local authorities and use the built-in Roblox report system.

Research and Awareness

Through a wide array of knowledge gathered by experience we aim to assist in the best way we can.

Resources

We know that staying informed is the first step toward protecting children online. Our growing library of resources offers clear, actionable advice for parents and children.

  • Roblox Report System: Use the built-in report system to report players in a fast and efficient manner.
  • Right to be: An organization we recommend for individuals who have been harassed online.
